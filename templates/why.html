About The Project

This website was created as the final project for MIS3640 Problem Solving and Software Design

The idea for this project initially came from a lack of inspiration from looking at food in my own fridge. This website is helpful when I have too much of one ingredient and no recipe in mind to use that specific ingredient. It is also helpful to search the nutrition facts for foods that don't always have labels such as fresh fruits, vegetables, and meat.

The main functions of this website are to input ingredients and get recipe recommendations based on the inputs, and to find nutrition facts for specific food inputs. The recipe recommendations provide the user with the title, measurements and ingredients, url to the recipe website, and recipe nutrition facts.

Any two ingredients can be used as inputs for the recipe search, and the food search can either be singular food items, or paired food items. For example, "chicken and rice" will return the nutrition facts for the combined consumption of those foods.
